Frequently Asked Questions

To learn more about RBA please complete the "Find Out More" form on the right or email us at information@royalbusinessacademy.org. The application process is very straight-forward and can be completed in just a few minutes. In addition to the online application form, you'll be asked to submit a photo of yourself, curriculum vitae, and a Statement of Intent. Complete the Online Application form to get started. We are selecting a maximum of 50 students for this unique program so we can best help you develop your business. Applications are reviewed in the order they are received. Your total cost for the program, including tuition, accommodations, food, and all other expenses associated with the program is EUR 1,000. SPECIAL NOTE: Our not-for-profit foundation has scholarships available for anyone who can't afford to pay. Please contact us to request a scholarship. The real cost for us to operate this program, including instruction, meals, accommodations, and all other related expenses, is more than EUR 9,000 per student. In future years, we may ask students to pay a larger portion of this cost to attend. However, this year, participants are asked only to pay EUR 1,000 and any who can't afford to pay can receive a scholarship. Why do we do this? Because we want to offer this program to EVERY young man and young woman of character and professional ability who wishes to become an outstanding leader in their community. How can we afford to do this? We know our program will help you be successful and we believe in you. We believe that if you follow the principles we teach, your business will be successful. In exchange for your ability to attend this program at a reduced price, we invite you to offer us a small amount of ownership in the business you create during the program. This way, our success is dependent on helping you be as successful as possible. We pay for ALL your expenses related to the program including tuition, educational materials, travel during the program, meals, and accommodations. Your only expenses will be for personal spending and for your travel to and from your home. Yes, all payments can be made by credit card through our secure web site. Although you will receive a formal certificate of completion as recognition of your accomplishment, the real crowning achievement of our program is for YOU to have your OWN SUCCESSFUL BUSINESS.
